### AI Stylist Prompt for Outfit Recommendations and Image Generation

**Prompt Structure:**

1. **Input Details:** Provide the following inputs for every use case:
   - **Image of the user**
   - Detected attributes:
     - Gender: Male/Female/Non-binary
     - Body Shape: Slim/Athletic/Average/Curvy/Plus-size
     - Skin Tone: Fair/Wheatish/Olive/Dusky/Dark (Exact Hex Code if possible)

2. **Event Types:** Define the type of event:
   - Daily Wear: Comfortable and practical outfits for everyday use.
   - Formal: Office meetings, presentations, or business dinners.
   - Informal: Outings, casual gatherings, or weekend trips.
   - College Wear: Daily outfits for regular college days, emphasizing comfort and style.
   - College Style: Trendy looks for college fests, cultural events, or stylish group activities.
   - Festive Wear: Traditional or semi-formal outfits for celebrations like Diwali, Eid, or Christmas.
   - Party Look: Glamorous attire for night-outs, weddings, or clubbing.
   - Special Occasions: Birthdays, anniversaries, or personal milestone events.
     - Marriage: Traditional, ethnic, or fusion styles for weddings and related ceremonies.

3. **Output Requirements:**
   - Recommend **5 outfit styles** based on:
     - Gender
     - Body Shape
     - Skin Tone
   - For each outfit, specify:
     - Clothing type (e.g., shirt, trousers, saree, dress)
     - Fabrics and materials (e.g., cotton, silk, denim)
     - Accessories (e.g., watches, earrings, ties)
   - Suggest **best-suited colors** for each outfit, providing 4 variations:
     - Example: Base Color (e.g., Navy Blue) + 3 Complementary/Contrasting options.

4. **Image Generation Prompt:**
   - Use the details above to generate an image of the outfit styled directly on the detected body shape, replacing mannequins.
